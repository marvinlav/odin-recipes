London Fog Latte

Description

A milk tea latte with Earl Grey as the base. Add steamed milk and lavender flavored syrup to elevate the drink.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Earl Grey Tea Bag
  • Temperature Probe to measure liquid temperature
  • Lavender Flavored Syrup

Recipe

  1. Add about 50 mL of hot water to steep the Earl Grey tea bag for 5 minutes
  2. Measure out 180 mL of milk
  3. Steam or Microwave the milk until it is around 60 degrees Celcius
  4. Combine Tea and Milk
  5. Add 1 spoon of Lavender Syrup (or to taste)
  6. Stir and serve.
