Hot Chocolate

Description

A sweet velvety drink for the sweet tooth. The steamed milk provides some texture.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Starbucks Hot Chocolate Powder Mix
  • Temperature Probe to measure liquid temperature

Recipe

  1. Measure out 180 mL of milk
  2. Add 28 grams of Starbucks Hot Chocolate Powder Mix to the milk
  3. Steam or Microwave the milk until it is around 60 degrees Celcius
  4. Stir and serve.
