Mocha

Description

A sweet velvety drink for the sweet tooth. Hot chocolate with a shot of espresso. The shot of espresso gives the dark, smokey flavors to contrast the sweetness of the chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Starbucks Hot Chocolate Powder Mix
  • Espresso Shot (from Espresso Machine) or Strong Coffee
  • Temperature Probe to measure liquid temperature

Recipe

  1. Measure out 180 mL of milk
  2. Add 28 grams of Starbucks Hot Chocolate Powder Mix to the milk
  3. Steam or Microwave the milk until it is around 60 degrees Celcius
  4. Add this milk and hot chocolate mix to the shot of espresso
  5. Stir and serve.
